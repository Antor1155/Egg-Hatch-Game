Egg Hatcher !!!

You are bullsEye

Hold mouse left button + move mouse on screen to move bullsEye

Click mouse left button on screen to move bullsEye to that place

Eggs will Regenarate at random time in random Places

Eggs are not vulnerable

After 3 seconds eggs will hatch to larva

Larva can be eaten by toad Enemies

Push the Larva/ Eggs to top bushes to save them from toads

Or push the toad away from Larvas

If larva reach top bushes successfully, you will see yellow Fireflies flying from the bushes


If larvas are eaten, will see blue blood sparkles


If 10 larvas are eaten, You loose

If you protect 50 larvas , YOU WIN !!!
